Our Classes

 

Discover the perfect class to help you reach your fitness goals, whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete. At Olympus Strength Gym, we offer a variety of classes designed to challenge and inspire you.

Strength Training

 

Build muscle and increase strength with our expert-led strength training classes. Whether you're new to lifting or experienced, our trainers will guide you through a variety of workouts designed to push your limits.

Cardio Fitness

 

Get your heart pumping with our high-energy cardio fitness classes. These sessions are perfect for burning calories and improving cardiovascular health through fun, dynamic workouts.

Yoga & Flexibility

 

Improve your flexibility, balance, and mental focus with our yoga classes. Suitable for all levels, our yoga sessions will leave you feeling refreshed and centered.

HIIT

 

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) is designed to torch fat and boost endurance in a short amount of time. These fast-paced sessions alternate between bursts of intense activity and rest periods.
